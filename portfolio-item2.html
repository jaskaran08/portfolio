This was a huge CMS project designed for an organisation to manage their employees information and agile method of work.

A content management system or SGC ( content management system or CMS in English) is a program for creating a website, a blog or even an online sales site. It allows the following functionalities:

  • It allows several individuals to work on the same document
  • It provides a publication chain ( workflow ) offering, for example, the possibility of putting the content of documents online
  • It makes it possible to separate the management operations of form and content
  • It allows content to be structured (use of FAQs , documents, blogs , discussion forums , etc.)

The Tech stack used for this site is PHP, MySQL, Javascript, HTML, CSS
